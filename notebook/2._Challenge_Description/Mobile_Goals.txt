====== Mobile Goals ======
Tuesday, May 02, 2017
AUTHOR: @cdunla6940

There are a total of 8 mobile goals in the VEX 2017-18 game, In the Zone. There are 4 mobile goals for the red alliance and 4 mobile goals for the blue alliance. These mobile goals can have regular cones stacked on top of it for 2 points. Placing these mobile goals in the 5 pt, 10 pt, and 20 pt. zones will give their respected amount
