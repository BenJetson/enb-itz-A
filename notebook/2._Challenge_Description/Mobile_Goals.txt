====== Mobile Goals ======
Tuesday, May 02, 2017
AUTHOR: @cdunla6940

There are a total of 8 mobile goals in the VEX 2017-18 game, In the Zone. There are 4 mobile goals for the red alliance and 4 mobile goals for the blue alliance. These mobile goals can have regular cones stacked on top of it for 2 points. Placing these mobile goals in the 5 pt, 10 pt, and 20 pt. zones will give their respected amount of points. These cones have a 5 inch radius and a 10 inch diameter.
the radius of the grounded part of the mobile goal is 3.55 inches, while the diameter is 7.10 inches. Thhe hieght of the mobile goal is 9.6 inches tall.
