====== Mobile Goals ======
Tuesday, May 02, 2017
AUTHOR: @cdunla6940

There are a total of 8 mobile goals on the field, 4 red and 4 blue.. Mobile goals are 9.6 inches high and have an outer diameter of 10 inchesCones can be stacked on top of these goals to earn points. Placing these mobile goals in the 5 pt, 10 pt, and 20 pt zones will yield the respective amount of points.
